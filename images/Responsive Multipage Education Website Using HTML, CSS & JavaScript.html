Frequently Asked Questions



How do I know the right courses for me?

"Our college emphasizes global student development, offering diverse courses, psychological assessments, mentorship, career counseling, cultural integration programs, and workshops tailored to individual interests and aspirations."

What programs or courses does the college offer?

We offer diverse programs in arts, science, technology, and business, designed to cater to global career aspirations and student interests.

What support services are available for students?

Our college provides academic advising, mental health counseling, mentorship, tutoring, and career services to ensure students thrive academically and personally.

What are the admission requirements?

Admission typically requires transcripts, recommendation letters, an application form, and sometimes test scores or an interview, ensuring a fair and inclusive selection process.

Are there scholarships or financial aid options?

We offer merit-based scholarships, need-based aid, and international student grants to make education accessible for all students.

What career opportunities are available for graduates?

We connect students with internships, global exchange programs, industry partnerships, and job placement services, ensuring strong career pathways after graduation.
